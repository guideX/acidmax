ByteCam 2.4

Description:

Broadcast and view live streaming video on IRC.  Use your WebCam or other video device to broadcast.  Built-in client can view an unlimited number of broadcasts.  Capture remote video and snapshots.



Installation:

1. Unzip the contents of this archive to your mIRC folder.
2. In mIRC, type /load -rs ByteCam.mrc


Usage:

ByteCam.mrc adds various menus in mIRC to control ByteCam.
